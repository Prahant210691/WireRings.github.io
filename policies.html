SAFETY POLICY

First Aid

At least 1 and up to 3 employees will have first aid knowledge. Minimum one first aid kit will be located in the factory premises.

The first aid kit will be inspected for inspected for sufficient contents.

Fire Prevention

No employees/visitors shall smoke in the factory premises.

Clean up any excess fuel spills, combustibles or flammable liquids at shaft floor.

Every employee must know the location of all fire extinguishing equipment in his/her work area.

Lockout

Many times during the day; ensure the machine is secure when the operator leaves the machine at breaks, lunchtime. Lockout is done whenever anyone has to conduct repairs, maintenance, cleaning or inspections to the machine.
